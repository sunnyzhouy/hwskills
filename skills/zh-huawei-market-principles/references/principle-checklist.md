# Principle Checklist

Use this checklist to review any market analysis.

## Core questions

- Is the market segmented in a way that changes resource allocation?
- Are target segments and scenarios explicit?
- Is the reason to win in each chosen segment clear?
- Is opportunity ranking based on value, fit, and risk?
- Are market conclusions connected to product or channel action?

## Signs of a strong draft

- It narrows the playing field.
- It explains why certain segments matter more.
- It makes downstream product or channel implications obvious.

## Signs of a weak draft

- It is a market facts dump.
- It uses size alone to rank opportunities.
- It never commits to target choices.
