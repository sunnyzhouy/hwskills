# Common Mistakes

Use this file when a market review sounds complete but does not support action.

## Common mistakes

- Segment by presentation categories rather than action categories
- Confuse market size with strategic fit
- Ignore customer scenario and buying context
- Produce no implications for product or channel

## Correction pattern

1. Redefine segments around distinct buying or usage patterns.
2. Choose target segments explicitly.
3. Rank opportunities by value, fit, and execution burden.
4. Derive product, sales, or service implications.
