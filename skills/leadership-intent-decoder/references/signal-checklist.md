# Signal Checklist

Use this file to decode what a boss, CEO, founder, or business head actually means.

## Core questions

- Is leadership emphasizing direction, pace, resource focus, or organizational discipline
- Which statements define boundaries versus ask for improvement
- What is explicit, and what is only inferred
- Is the real dissatisfaction about results, path, execution quality, or thinking model
- Do these signals change strategy, market choice, product plans, or org action

## Signs of a strong decode

- It separates explicit statements from inference
- It identifies what actually changed in priority
- It turns speech into product, market, or org implications
- It marks what still needs confirmation

## Signs of a weak decode

- It only summarizes the source material
- It mistakes rhetoric or tone for direction
- It treats every statement as top priority
- It produces no downstream action implications
