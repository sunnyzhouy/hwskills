# Workflow

Use this file when the user needs the full strategic planning flow.

## Full sequence

1. Clarify the business boundary and planning horizon.
2. Describe the external change and internal performance gap.
3. Name the few strategic choices that matter.
4. Translate those choices into annual objectives and major initiatives.
5. Define governance, review cadence, and risk assumptions.
6. Package the result into the requested artifact.

## Facilitation prompts

- What changed in the market that makes the old logic insufficient?
- What must the business be better at to win?
- Which few bets matter most this year?
- What must not be pursued?
- Which metric tells us early that the plan is working?
