# Workflow

Use this file when the user needs the full market management flow.

## Full sequence

1. Define the market boundary and segmentation logic.
2. Select target segments and scenarios.
3. Compare opportunities by attractiveness, fit, and risk.
4. Derive implications for strategy, product, channel, or service.
5. Package the findings into a decision-ready artifact.

## Facilitation prompts

- Which segment changes our resource allocation the most?
- Why do customers in this segment buy or switch?
- Where is the market attractive but strategically weak for us?
- What does this imply for roadmap or channel focus?
