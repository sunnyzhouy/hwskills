# Principle Checklist

Use this checklist to review any strategy draft.

## Core questions

- Is the strategic intent explicit and bounded?
- Is the external challenge concrete rather than generic?
- Are the few decisive choices named?
- Are capability gaps explicit?
- Is the annual plan derived from strategic choices?
- Are metrics, owners, and review cadence present?

## Signs of a strong draft

- It explains why these choices win.
- It shows what not to do.
- It names assumptions and risks.
- It connects strategic themes to measurable annual control points.

## Signs of a weak draft

- It is a wish list.
- It confuses projects with strategy.
- It uses slogans instead of mechanism.
- It lists metrics without ownership or timing.
