# Principle Checklist

Use this checklist to review roadmap and release plans.

## Core questions

- Is the roadmap tied to strategy and customer scenarios?
- Are release themes coherent?
- Are priorities explicit?
- Are tradeoffs and dependencies visible?
- Is the handoff to development decision-ready?

## Signs of a strong draft

- It makes clear what ships when and why.
- It reveals what will not be done.
- It connects roadmap logic to business and customer outcomes.

## Signs of a weak draft

- It is a backlog disguised as a roadmap.
- It hides tradeoffs.
- It lacks release themes or dependency logic.
