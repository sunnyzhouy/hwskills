# Principle Checklist

Use this checklist to review service operating designs.

## Core questions

- Is issue intake classifiable and actionable?
- Are ownership and escalation explicit?
- Is closure defined by customer resolution?
- Are feedback loops to product or quality clear?
- Are service metrics tied to operational outcomes?

## Signs of a strong draft

- It separates issue types clearly.
- It defines real decision owners.
- It closes the loop into product and management.

## Signs of a weak draft

- It optimizes ticket movement rather than resolution.
- It has no real escalation authority.
- It tracks activity but not service quality.
