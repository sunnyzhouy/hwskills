# Method Intake Template

## Problem

- What decision is the user actually trying to make?
- What business unit, product, or region is in scope?

## Timing

- Is this a strategic horizon, annual horizon, release horizon, or project horizon?

## Output

- Does the user need a memo, deck outline, roadmap, review checklist, or operating model?

## Constraints

- What is fixed: revenue target, launch date, team size, compliance, or channel?
